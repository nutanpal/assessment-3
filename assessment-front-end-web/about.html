About my Neighbourhood

Plano, Texas

Plano is a city in North Texas that has been named by Forbes Magazine as one of America’s best places to live. It is the fourth largest in the Dallas Fort Worth region home to several fortune 1000 companies. Plano is famous for our smart people, amazing quality of life, and strong job market. Gaining popularity as having an excellent education system, amazing neighborhoods."

It is excellent for families with kids and young professionals alike with a superb Recreation infrastructure including all governmental agencies/departments.

Parks & Playgrounds

If you are an outdoor person we have got you covered. Plano has many amazing parkland and greenspace to host. Parks are within 10 min drive to any resident. The largest park in the city is Haggard Park. 800-acre park which has 3.5 miles of concrete trails and 5 miles of soft trails. It is a perfect place to go and reconnect with nature.

School and Universities

Colleges

There are about 23 colleges in the area, including 19 private colleges and universities, 3 public colleges and universities, and 1 community college offering 2-year degrees.

Schools

Within 73 campuses over 100 square miles, over 7,000 Plano ISD teachers and staff serve kids from 127 countries with 95 spoken languages. Plano ISD also has 4 specialized facilities: Plano Academy High School, an IB World School and the Health Science Academy (Plano East Senior High School), and an IB PYP World Elementary School at Huffman.Use the Plano ISD School Finder to see which one you are zoned to.
